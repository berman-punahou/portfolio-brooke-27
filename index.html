About Me

My Life

My name is Brooke Teruya and I am 15 years old. I was born in California, but my family is from Hawaii. We moved back when I was 3 years old.

Family

I live with my my mom, dad, and 14 year old sister. My sister's name is Kayla and we are one and a half years apart in age. She is currently a freshman at HBA.

Education

I came to Punahou in the 6th grade and am currently a sophomore. Before coming to Punahou, I went to Wilson Elementary. I love Punahou because of the opportunites that are offered to students and the friends that I have made.

Hobbies

Some hobbies that I have are reading, dancing, and hanging at the beach. Reading has always been important to me and I've loved reading ever since I was little. I started dancing when I was 3, and it's been an off and on career ever since. Right now, I dance hip-hop and jazz, both genres that I love. Although I'm not the biggest fan of sand, I love tanning and playing in the water with my friends. I've learned to appreciate the beaches we have here in Hawaii, and want to take advantage of them. I also like going hiking or running around the island. It can be so nice to enjoy the views Hawaii has to offer, and it's a great way to stay in shape.
